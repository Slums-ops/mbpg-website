STRATEGIC ECONOMIC INVESTMENTS

Agriculture, MSMEs, Fisheries, Tourism

GOAL:

Drive economic growth through sustainable agribusiness, fisheries, tourism, and small-medium enterprises (SMEs).

PROJECTS:

  • Expansion of oil palm, vanilla, and cocoa farming
  • Strengthening fisheries and aquaculture for increased seafood exports.
  • Establishing eco-tourism hubs in the Trobriand Islands and Louisiade Archipelago.
  • Supporting MSMEs through financial grants and training.

PROGRESS & UPDATES:

  • New agriculture cooperatives established for cocoa and vanilla farmers.
  • Tourism industry revival post-COVID with new resort developments.
  • Increased seafood exports, especially tuna and shellfish farming.
