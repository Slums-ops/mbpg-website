INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENT

Roads, Bridges, Wharves, ICT, Energy

GOAL:

Improve transportation, communication, and energy access to enhance connectivity and productivity.

PROJECTS:

  • Rehabilitation of the Alotau-Gurney Highway and rural road networks.
  • Upgrading wharves and jetties to boost inter-island trade.
  • Expansion of solar and hydro energy projects for rural electrification.
  • ICT rollout to improve internet access across the province.

PROGRESS & UPDATES:

  • 50km of rural roads upgraded in 2023.
  • Hybrid solar-diesel power plants established in remote islands.
  • Increased mobile and internet coverage in previously unconnected communities.
